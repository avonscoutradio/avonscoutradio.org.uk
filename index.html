Welcome to Avon Scout Radio

Hi, we're Avon Scout Radio, a Scouts Active Support Unit based in Bristol, UK.

We provide a variety of services to support broadcast radio in Scouts, such as badgework, live radio from your events and media support.

We also look after JOTI Radio, the official radio station for World Scouting's biggest event: JOTA-JOTI.
